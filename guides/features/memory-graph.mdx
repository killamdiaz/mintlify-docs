### **Search bar (top)**

- Start typing a keyword (example: AE) and Atlas Engine will show **matching nodes** in a dropdown list.
- Results are labeled by **type** (for example, **DOCUMENT**) so you know what youâ€™re jumping into.

### **Results dropdown**

### **What happens when you select a result**

When you click a result from the list:

- The graph **focuses/centers** on that node
- You can immediately see its **connections and relationships** in context (linked memories, similar docs, shared pools, etc.)

### **Quick controls next to search**

Near the search bar you also have graph controls (commonly used for):
